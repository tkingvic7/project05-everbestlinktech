Everbest Link Technology is a precision machining and moulding manufacturer based in Kulim and the greater Penang region. We bring deep technical expertise and a results-driven approach to every project.

From rapid prototyping to high-volume production, our experienced team works closely with clients to deliver tight-tolerance components that meet the highest industry standards.

What We Do

Our Core
Services

We specialize in a range of high-precision manufacturing services, combining advanced machinery with skilled craftsmanship to deliver components that meet exact specifications every time.

01
Wire Cut & CNC Machining

High-precision wire EDM cutting and CNC machining for complex geometries, tight tolerances, and a wide range of materials including tool steels and aluminium.

02
PCB Mould Development

Custom PCB mould design and development for the electronics industry, engineered to the finest dimensional accuracy and surface finish requirements.

03
Metal Stamping Mould

Precision metal stamping mould development optimized for consistent repeatability, long tool life, and efficient high-volume production runs.

04
Jig & Fixture Development

Custom jigs and fixtures designed to improve accuracy, repeatability, and throughput in assembly and machining operations across all production scales.

Specialized Capability
Superdrill Services
High-speed deep-hole drilling for complex workpieces requiring precision bore profiles.
